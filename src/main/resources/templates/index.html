Reimagining spaces.

Intro

Every day, students are bombarded with pamphlets, flyers and other forms of advertising that swarm their spaces while offering little functional value. SupplyStop aims to solve this problem by empowering organizations and businesses with practical exposure; the handouts become tools, not liabilities. Their logos will be printed on various bulk merchandise: pens, dry-erase markers, cups, tissue packs, ping-pong balls, etc and be offered from a single point of distribution on campus: the SupplyStop. In this way, students are not only willfully choosing the ads they are seeing, but are using them in their workspaces for an extended period of time as well.
